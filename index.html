Our minigames, we're sure you'll love them!

Kits

Our skywars server has kits. Kits are extra abilities you get when your game starts. You can select a kit before the game starts. You can buy kits with ingame coins or you can buy coins in the gamecraft shop. (see link to shop)

Skywars

Skywars is a popular Minecraft minigame with a simple premise: fight until one player (or team) is left alive. The catch is that you begin on a small island. It's up to you to build bridges to attack other players, or walls to defend yourself.

Maps

In skywars you have multiple maps to play in. Every map has it's own title and map specific islands. You have for example the map candy which contains islands with candy on it. We created a large variation so you can choose what you like to play.
